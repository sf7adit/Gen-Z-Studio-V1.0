About Gen-Z Studio

Gen-Z Studio is a creator management agency founded by Adit Kumar Sarkar. Our goal is to help emerging creators connect with brands through professional communication, sponsorship outreach, campaign coordination, and ethical creator representation. We believe in transparency, long-term relationships, and building opportunities honestly—without making promises we cannot guarantee.

How It Works

1. Apply

Creators or brands reach out through our contact form.

2. Initial Discussion

We discuss goals, expectations, and whether we're a good fit.

3. Onboarding

If both sides agree, we begin the onboarding process.

4. Brand Outreach

We identify relevant brands and begin professional outreach.

5. Negotiation

We support discussions to help reach mutually beneficial agreements.

6. Campaign Management

We help coordinate communication throughout the campaign.

7. Growth

We aim to build long-term professional relationships over time.
